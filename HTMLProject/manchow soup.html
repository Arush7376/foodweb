Manchow Soup

Manchurian is a popular Indo-Chinese dish that consists of deep-fried vegetables or meatballs tossed in a flavorful, tangy, and slightly sweet sauce. There are variations of Manchurian, such as vegetable Manchurian, chicken Manchurian, and gobi (cauliflower) Manchurian. Here's a general recipe for vegetable Manchurian:

Ingredients:

For Vagetable Balls:

  1. 1 cup finely chopped mixed vegetables (cabbage, carrot, bell peppers, beans)
  2. 1/4 cup finely chopped spring onions
  3. 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  4. 1/4 cup cornflour
  5. 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  6. 1 tsp soy sauce
  7. 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  8. Salt to taste
  9. Oil for deep frying

Steps:

For Manchurian Sauce:

  1. 2 tbsp oil
  2. 1 tbsp finely chopped garlic
  3. 1 tbsp finely chopped ginger
  4. 1/2 cup finely chopped spring onions
  5. 1/4 cup finely chopped capsicum (bell pepper)
  6. 2 tbsp soy sauce
  7. 1 tbsp chili sauce
  8. 1 tsp vinegar
  9. 1 tsp sugar
  10. Salt to taste
  11. 1 cup water
  12. 1.5 tbsp cornflour mixed with 3 tbsp water (to make a slurry)

instructions:

For Vagetable Balls:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the finely chopped vegetables, spring onions, all-purpose flour, cornflour, ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, black pepper powder, and salt.
  2. Form small balls from the mixture. If it's too dry, you can add a little water to bind the ingredients.
  3. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying. Once the oil is hot, fry the vegetable balls until they turn golden brown. Remove and set aside.

For Manchurian Sauce:

  1. In a separate pan, heat 2 tbsp of oil.
  2. Add chopped garlic and ginger. Saut√© for a minute until they become aromatic.

  6. Slowly add the cornflour slurry while continuously stirring the sauce. This will help thicken the sauce.
  7. Once the sauce reaches the desired consistency, add the fried vegetable balls. Toss them in the sauce until well coated.
  8. Garnish with chopped spring onions.

Serve vegetable Manchurian hot as an appetizer or with fried rice/noodles as a main course. Adjust the spice levels and seasoning according to your taste preferences.
