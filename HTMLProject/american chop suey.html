American Chop Suey

Ingredients:

  • 200g noodles
  • 1 cup thinly sliced cabbage
  • 1 cup thinly sliced bell peppers (mix of colors)
  • 1 cup thinly sliced carrots
  • 1 cup thinly sliced onions
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan or wok, heat oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add onions and stir-fry until translucent.
  4. Add carrots, bell peppers, and cabbage. Stir-fry until the vegetables are tender yet crisp.
  5. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, ketchup, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  6. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir well.
  7. Add the cooked noodles and toss everything together until well combined.
  8. Adjust seasoning if needed. Serve hot.
