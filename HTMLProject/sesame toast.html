Sesame Toast

Sesame toast is a delicious and popular dish that typically consists of bread slices coated with a flavorful mixture and toasted until golden brown. Here's a basic recipe for making sesame toast:

Ingredients:

  1. Bread slices (white or whole wheat)
  2. 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  3. 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  4. 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  5. 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  6. 1 clove garlic, minced (optional)
  7. Salt and pepper to taste
  8. Chopped fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the Sesame Mixture: In a bowl, combine the melted butter, sesame seeds, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, minced garlic (if using), salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly combined.
  3. Spread the Mixture: Lay the bread slices on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Using a brush or a spoon, spread the sesame mixture evenly over one side of each bread slice. Make sure to coat the bread all the way to the edges.
  4. Toast in the Oven: Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and toast the bread for about 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown and crispy. Keep an eye on them to prevent burning.
  5. Garnish (Optional): If desired, garnish the sesame toast with chopped fresh parsley for added color and flavor.
  6. Serve: Once the sesame toast is toasted to perfection, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a minute or two. Cut the slices into smaller pieces if you'd like and serve them warm.

Enjoy your delicious sesame toast! This recipe is versatile, and you can adjust the ingredients to suit your taste preferences. You can also experiment with adding other seasonings or herbs to customize the flavor.
